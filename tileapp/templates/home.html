Quadratic Tile Patterns

Algebra tiles are great tools for teaching students how to look for patterns and make predictions.
For instance, a great task would be to ask students to draw the missing figures and write a general equation describing how the pattern changes.

This app has been designed to make the creation of such patterns easy so you don't have to manually draw the tiles. With a simple text-based input to represent algebra tiles, you can quickly roll out worksheets for students to work with. The only restriction is that unit side lengths cannot be matched with variable side lengths. (i.e. 'O.' is invalid)

  • Unit Tile: '.'
  • Linear Tile: '-' or '|'
  • Quadratic Tile: 'O'
